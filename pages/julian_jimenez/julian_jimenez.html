ScriptEd Interns 2018

Julian Jimenez

Intern At Oscar Health

About Me

I attended Uncommon Charter High School between the years of 2014 and 2018 and I will be attending Dartmouth College from the years 2018 to 2022. I will be pusuing a degree in computer science.

My Internship Experience

As a hispanic teen new to the tech industry, I have been exposed to the workplace and have an idea of what to expect when I graduate from college and get a job. It has been wonderful being able to experience an inclusive workplace where I don't feel out of place as a young intern. I have learned how a tech company operates and how many different people with different skills come together to collaborate and create.

What I Worked On

I was tasked with creating a web application that allows users to contact Oscar Health services with their mental health needs. Me and my coworker created an interactive interface utilizing ReactJS in order to fill out surveys corresponding to what mental condition they are experiencing and put them in contact with the Oscar Health concierge service.

Key Takeaways

As a young software engineer it is important to remember that people are always happy to help you. Initially I struggled with asking for help when I was blocked but learned to communicate with people across different teams to ask them for help with React and help resolve issues with git commands. If I am to take anything away from interning at Oscar Health it is that tech companies are very collaborative and inclusive.
