About Me

I grew up in Midtown Manhattan and graduated from Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Arts and Performing Arts. I will be attending Hunter College at CUNY in the fall studying Computer Science. On my leisure time, I enjoy drawing, reading, and working on personal projects whenever the inspiration hits.

My Internship Experience

I had the opportunity to meet some of the most talented and unique people who were increadibly welcoming and supportive throughout the entire internship. I also had the honor of meeting some of the best pets in the world: Trudy, Rocky and Neptune.

What I Worked On

I worked with Group Nine's Emerging Platforms team to create their new website based on a UI design by another intern using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, utilizing CSS grids and JQuery.

Key Takeaways

One of the most important things I learned was to take control of your own workload - don't try to take on too much but also to take the initiative to ask for more work if necessary. Another would be to prioritize your work - don't try to finish everything at once but rather piece by piece.
