Key Takeaways

    Even in a casual workplace, always ask if you're allowed to do something. In work communities, going to a new one means you have to see what the limits of your actions. Once you learn that, you could just start work as soon as possible if you had an idea on what you wanted to work on. If not use a messager or go to someone so that you can speak to someone if they have work for you.
    Find other people than your manager to ask for help from. Your manager isn't always going to be available. Plus, getting to know each other means it becomes less awkward to work together since they are just strangers. None of you would know how you act normally so they could act normally in turn. A comfortable talk with each other can keep things focused.
